1. The study


You are invited to participate in a study on economic decisions. We greatly appreciate your participation. This study is conducted by Matthieu Jost, Marianne Schmid Mast from the University of Lausanne, and Gwendolin Sajons from ESCP Berlin.



2. Outline of the study procedure


If you agree to participate, we ask you to accomplish two tasks.

  • First, you will watch 6 videos.
  • Second, you will take a decision based on the six videos.

You will need about 30 minutes to complete the study.



3. Remuneration


At the end of the study, you will be remunerated in the following way:

  • You will be awarded £3 for your participation..
  • You may earn an additional bonus that will depend on your decision. This bonus may vary between £0 à £27.

The total amount you will receive will thus be between £3 and £30.

Please note that we will check whether you have access to the audio of your device, and the comprehension of the task. If you fail either of the checks, your participation will be cancelled, and you will receive £0. The checks will be at the beginning of the study.



4. Confidentiality and data processing


Your answers will be recorded by Matthieu Jost of HEC Lausanne, University of Lausanne, in a confidential and secure way.

During this study, no personally identifiable information will be collected. Your participation in this study is anonymous.

The confidentiality of the data is guaranteed.



5. Your rights


Your participation is completely voluntary and you are completely free to stop the study at any time, without notice. In case you stop, you will receive £0 and the data you have provided will be deleted.



6. Participation conditions to the study


By signing this form, you acknowledge that you have read the above information and that you agree to it.
